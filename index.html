Bio

I am a first-year PhD student at the Soolab in ShanghaiTech University advised by Prof. Sibei Yang. Previously, I obtained my Bachelor's degree from the SouthWest University in 2021. My research interests lie in computer vision, natural language processing, and their intersection. My current research focuses on Vision-Language Understanding and Large Vision-Language Models.
